The Team

Dr. Paige Rodeghero

Paige Rodeghero is an Assistant Professor in the School of Computing, Computer Science Division, at Clemson University where she leads the HFSE research group. She received her Ph.D. from the University of Notre Dame, where she was advised by Dr. Collin McMillan. Her current research is in the area of software engineering, program comprehension, evolution and maintenance, testing, and software bots. She received the ACM Distinguished Paper Award at ICSE 2014. She has published many top tier conference and journal papers. In her spare time, she teaches barre workouts at a local wellness studio and hikes. See her professional website for additional information.

PhD Students

Brock Tubre

Brock Tubre is a PhD student who joined the HFSE team in the Spring of 2019. He began the program with a Bachelor's of Computer Science from Louisiana Tech University and a Master's in Computer Science from Louisiana State University Shreveport. Brock is an experienced software developer and technical instructor currently instructing at A Cloud Guru. Before joining the HFSE team, he worked on enterprise software solutions for the US government. Building software and teaching the world about cloud computing are his professional passions. When he is not researching, developing or teaching, he enjoys speed solving puzzle cubes (even blind solving), homebrewing craft beer and playing baseball.
